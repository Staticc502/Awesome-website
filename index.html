Froggy Info

Frogs love mushrooms and spend lots of time with them.

Sometimes frogs will perch upon a mushroom to get a better vantage point for sightseeing.

Frogs will even use mushrooms as protection from the rain and snow.

Frogs and mushrooms live harmoniously together!
